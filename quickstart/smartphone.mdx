---

Smartphone tests drive a real Android or iOS device, tapping, typing, and swiping through a native app just like a user. They run on a Smartphone agent. Here's your first one.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Create the test.** Open the **Tests** section for your product, click **Create**, and choose the **Smartphone** channel. Name the test and set the app to launch by its package name (Android) or bundle ID (iOS).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Add steps.** Build the flow with smartphone actions, tap an element, fill an input, swipe, wait, and assert that something is displayed. Save the test.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Run it on a device.** Click **Run** and pick a connected smartphone. Watch execution live under **Executions**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Read the result.** Open the execution to see status, per-step screenshots, and timing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
