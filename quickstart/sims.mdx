Cellular tests run on physical SIM cards inside a Cellular agent, sending and receiving SMS, navigating USSD menus, and checking network registration. Here's your first SIM test.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Create the test.** Open the **Tests** section for your product, click **Create**, and choose the **Cellular** channel. Name the test.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Add steps.** Build the flow with cellular actions, send a USSD command and assert the response, or send and receive an SMS. Save the test.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Run it on a SIM.** Click **Run** and pick a SIM in a Cellular agent slot. Watch execution live under **Executions**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Read the result.** Open the execution to see status, the USSD or SMS responses, and timing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
