---
title: Swap a SIM in a cellular robot
description: Replace a SIM card and reconfigure its slot in the platform.
---

Swapping a SIM is a mix of physical and platform work: put the robot in maintenance, change the card, then update the slot configuration so tests target the right number.

<Warning>
  Every SIM card used in a robot must be configured **without a PIN code**.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the robot and put it in maintenance">
    In the **Administration** section, choose **Cellular robots**.

    <Frame caption="The Administration section">
      ![Administration section](/images/onpremise/swap-sim-01.webp)
    </Frame>

    <Frame caption="The cellular robots list">
      ![Cellular robots](/images/onpremise/swap-sim-02.webp)
    </Frame>

    Click the three dots on the robot's box and select **Set agent in maintenance**.

    <Frame caption="Set the agent in maintenance">
      ![Set agent in maintenance](/images/onpremise/swap-sim-03.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Swap the card">
    Withdraw the current SIM and install the new one (no PIN code).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconfigure the slot">
    Click the robot's three dots, select **Edit**, and browse to the **slots** section.

    <Frame caption="The slots section of the robot">
      ![Slots section](/images/onpremise/swap-sim-04.webp)
    </Frame>

    Click the three dots left of the slot and select **Reboot this slot**. After a minute, click them again and select **Edit**.

    <Frame caption="Reboot, then edit the slot">
      ![Reboot this slot](/images/onpremise/swap-sim-05.webp)
    </Frame>

    Update the values for the new SIM: **National msisdn**, **International msisdn** (without `+`), and **Phone prefix**. Assign the necessary products, add the APN data if needed, and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reboot and release">
    From the slots section, reboot the slot once more.

    <Frame caption="Final slot reboot">
      ![Final reboot](/images/onpremise/swap-sim-06.webp)
    </Frame>

    Back in the cellular robots menu, release the robot with **Unset agent in maintenance**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
