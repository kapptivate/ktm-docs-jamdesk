Some robot models have no display output. To interact with them, you connect over a serial line instead of plugging a screen. This concerns the mini cellular robot:

<Frame caption="The mini cellular robot">
  ![Mini cellular robot](/images/onpremise/headless-login-01.webp)
</Frame>

## What you need

- A **serial-to-USB adapter**:

<Frame caption="Serial to USB adapter">
  ![Serial to USB adapter](/images/onpremise/headless-login-02.webp)
</Frame>

- The adapter's **driver** installed on your computer (ask support for the download link for your adapter model).
- A serial terminal application, for example **CoolTerm** (available for macOS, Windows, and Linux).

## Connect

Configure the serial connection with:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Baudrate | 115200 |
| Data bits | 8 |
| Parity | None |
| Stop bit | 1 |

Open the connection: you get the robot's console and can log in with your credentials.
