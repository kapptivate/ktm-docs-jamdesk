Supervision overview gives you a high-level summary of monitoring status across all your products. You see uptime counters, execution counts, and a status timeline showing how each product has performed over your chosen time range.

## Overview summary

The overview displays all your monitored products with status indicators. Four counter cards at the top show key metrics:

- **Products monitored**: number of products with active monitors.
- **Tests running**: total count of tests currently active.
- **Current uptime**: percentage of successful executions.
- **Day-over-day change**: how metrics compare to the previous 24 hours.

These counters update as you change the time range or select a product. Click any product in the list to drill into detailed metrics and a status timeline.

## Product details view

Select a product to see its detailed metrics. The product details view shows:

- **Counter cards** for the selected product (uptime %, execution count, error count).
- **Day-over-day comparison** showing the same metrics from the previous 24-hour period, with change indicators.
- **Status timeline** visualizing execution status segments chronologically. Each colored segment represents a period with its status (success, failed, running, pending, or no results).
- **Status legend** as a reference guide for timeline colors and icons.

Use the breadcrumb to navigate back to the overview list.

## Time range controls

Choose a start and end date for your view. The default is the last 24 hours. Metrics for the previous 24-hour period are fetched automatically for comparison; you don't need to configure this separately.

Aggregation interval controls how data is bucketed in the timeline (default: 30 minutes). Smaller intervals show more detail; larger intervals smooth out short fluctuations.

Optionally filter by test to focus on a specific test within the selected product.
