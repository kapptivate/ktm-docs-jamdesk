Notifications alert you when robots become unavailable. Configure alert detection periods and choose where to receive alerts -- email, SMS, Slack, Teams, or webhooks.

## What you can do

- [**Enable robot availability monitoring**](/administration/notifications/availability) -- track when robots go offline and set how long they can be down before triggering an alert.
- [**Configure notification channels**](/administration/notifications/channels) -- set up email, SMS, Slack, Teams, and webhook delivery for your alerts.

Once monitoring is enabled, Kapptivate requires at least one active channel before you can save. For details on the incidents these alerts generate, see [incidents](/monitoring/incidents). To authenticate the external services your channels connect to, see [API keys](/administration/api-keys).
