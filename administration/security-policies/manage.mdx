---
title: Manage security policies
description: Assign users, clone policies, and set a workspace default.
---

After creating and configuring a security policy, assign users to it, clone it as a starting point for new policies, or mark it as the workspace default.

## Assign users to a policy

The **Users** section at the bottom of the policy form shows who the policy applies to. A new policy shows *"No users assigned to this policy yet."*

Click **Add Users**, select users from the modal (search by name, username, or email), and confirm. To remove a user, click the delete button next to their name.

## Clone a policy

Cloning copies a policy as a starting point for a new one. Open the **⋮** menu on a policy's row, choose **Clone**, then modify the copy as needed. The cloned policy is created as a custom (non-default) policy.

## Set a default policy

Mark a policy as the default for your workspace — the **Default** badge in the list shows which one is active. New users are automatically assigned the default policy unless you assign them to a different one.

Only one policy can be the default at a time.
