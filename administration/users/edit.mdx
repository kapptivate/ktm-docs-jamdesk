---
title: Edit a user
description: Update a user's profile, team assignments, product access, roles, and security policy.
---

You can update any user's profile, teams, products, roles, and security policy at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

## Update user details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the user">
    Go to **Administration > Users** and click the user's name in the table to open their edit page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your changes">
    Update any combination of:
    - **Profile** -- username, name, email
    - **Teams** -- add or remove team memberships
    - **Product access** -- add or remove direct product assignments and roles
    - **Security policy** -- switch to a different policy
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save Changes** to apply your updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can make individual changes without affecting other properties. For example, updating a user's email does not change their team assignments.
