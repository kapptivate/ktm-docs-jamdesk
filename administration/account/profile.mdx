---
title: Manage your profile
description: Update your name, username, and view your email address.
---

Your profile contains your name, username, and email address. You can edit your name and username, but your email is read-only within your profile page.

To update your profile:

1. Click your user icon at the top right of the sidebar
2. Select "My account"
3. Scroll to the Profile section
4. Edit your first name, last name, or username
5. Click "Save Changes"

Your username must be unique. The system checks availability as you type, pausing briefly before validating to avoid redundant checks.
