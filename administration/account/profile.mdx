title: Manage your profile
description: Update your name, username, and view your email address.
---

Your profile contains your name, username, and email address. You can edit your name and username, but your email is read-only within your profile page.

To update your profile:

1. Click your user icon at the top right of the sidebar
2. Select "My account"
3. Scroll to the Profile section
4. Edit your first name, last name, or username
5. Click "Save Changes"

Your username must be unique. The system checks availability as you type, pausing briefly before validating to avoid redundant checks.

![The Profile section, editing username, first name, and last name with a read-only email field](/images/account-profile.webp)

## Personal access token

Your profile also holds a **personal access token** — a credential tied to your account that authenticates external tools like the [Kapptivate CLI](/cli/overview) and the [MCP server](/mcp/overview). Treat it like a password.

To use it, open the Profile section, reveal the token, and copy it into the tool you're configuring. The token is regenerated when you log out, so you'll need to copy it again after your next login.

![The personal access token field in the Profile section, with a reveal-and-copy control](/images/account-pat.webp)
