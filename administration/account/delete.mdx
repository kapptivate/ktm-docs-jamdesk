---
title: Delete your account
description: Permanently delete your Kapptivate account and what that removes.
---

![The Delete Account control in the Account & Password section, with a permanent-deletion warning](/images/account-delete.webp)

You can permanently delete your own account from the **Account & Password** section of My account. This removes your account and the personal data attached to it.

<Danger>
  Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Your account and its associated data are erased and can't be restored.
</Danger>

To delete your account:

1. Click your user icon at the top right of the sidebar
2. Select "My account"
3. Scroll to "Account & Password"
4. Click "Delete Account"
5. Confirm in the dialog

If you only need new credentials, [change your password](/administration/account/password) instead. To remove someone else from the workspace, an administrator does that from [Users](/administration/users), not here.
