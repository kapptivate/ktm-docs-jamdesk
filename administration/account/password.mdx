---
title: Change your password
description: Reset your account password and understand what happens after the change.
---

![The Reset password modal asking for the current password and the new password twice](/images/account-password.webp)

You can reset your password from your profile page at any time.

1. Click your workspace name at the top of the sidebar and select **Settings**, then open **Profile** under My account
2. Scroll to **Account & Password**
3. Click **Change password**
4. In the "Reset password" modal, enter your current password, then your new password twice
5. Click **Reset password**

Changing your password logs you out automatically and returns you to the login page. Log back in with your new password.
