---
title: Change your password
description: Reset your account password and understand what happens after the change.
---

You can reset your password from your account page at any time.

1. Go to My account
2. Scroll to "Account and Password"
3. Click "Reset Password"
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter your new password and confirm it
6. Click "Confirm"

After you change your password, you'll be logged out automatically in 2 seconds and returned to the login page. You can then log back in with your new password.
