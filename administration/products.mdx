A product is an app, website, or service you test within your workspace. It acts as the top-level container that groups together your tests, devices, teams, and monitoring configuration.

## What you can do

- [**Create a product**](/administration/products/create): pick an icon, a name, and optionally assign teams and devices right away.
- [**Edit a product**](/administration/products/edit): update details, manage team access, or change device assignments after creation.
- [**Assign teams**](/administration/products/teams): grant all members of a team access to the product in one step.
- [**Add devices**](/administration/products/devices): link smartphones, SIM cards, and ethernet agents so they can run tests on this product.
- [**Configure monitoring**](/administration/products/monitoring): enable device health tracking and choose an aggregation interval.
- [**View and filter**](/administration/products/view): browse, sort, and export your products list.

Products sit at the center of your workspace. [Teams](/administration/teams) give users access to a product, [devices](/administration/products/devices) run tests against it, and [monitors](/monitoring/overview) track its availability over time.
