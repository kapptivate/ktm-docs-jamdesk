A product is an app, website, or service you test within your workspace. This page covers creating and editing products, assigning teams and devices to them, and configuring product-level settings.

## Create a product

Products serve as the top-level container for all testing and monitoring activity. When you create a product, you can assign teams and devices right away.

To create a new product:

1. From the workspace navigation, go to Administration > Products
2. Click "Create product"
3. In the About panel:
   - Select an icon from the predefined set (50+ icons available)
   - Choose an icon color from the palette
   - Enter a product name
   - The slug is auto-generated from your product name in kebab-case and cannot be changed
4. Optionally add teams and devices now (you can also add them later when editing)
5. Click "Create"

Your new product appears immediately in the products list and is ready for testing.

## Edit a product

You can edit any product to update its details, add or remove teams and devices, or configure monitoring settings.

To edit a product:

1. Go to Administration > Products
2. Click the product name to open the edit page
3. Make your changes in the relevant sections:
   - **About** — Change the icon, color, or product name
   - **Teams** — Add or remove teams with access to this product
   - **Devices** — Associate smartphones, SIM cards, and ethernet agents with the product
   - **Settings** — Enable monitoring and set the aggregation interval

4. Click "Save Changes"

<Info>
Your product slug is set when you create the product and cannot be changed. The slug is a URL-friendly identifier used in product-specific URLs.
</Info>

## Assign teams to a product

Team members need product access to work with a product. You can assign teams to grant all members access at once.

1. Open the product for editing
2. Scroll to the Teams section
3. Click "Add teams"
4. In the modal, select one or more teams
5. Click "Confirm"
6. Click "Save Changes"

Team members inherit this product access automatically.

## Add devices to a product

Devices are the phones, SIM cards, and agents you use for testing. Linking a device to a product makes it available for tests on that product.

1. Open the product for editing
2. Scroll to the Devices section
3. Click "Add devices"
4. In the modal, select devices from the list (search by name or ID to find them faster)
5. Click "Confirm"
6. Click "Save Changes"

You can assign smartphones, SIM cards, and ethernet agents (web devices) to the same product.

## Configure product monitoring

Device monitoring captures system health and performance metrics for devices assigned to your product. You can enable or disable monitoring and set how frequently data is aggregated.

1. Open the product for editing
2. Scroll to Settings (visible only when editing an existing product)
3. Toggle monitoring on or off
4. Set the aggregation interval in minutes (e.g., 5, 15, or 30)
5. Click "Save Changes"

The aggregation interval determines how often monitoring data is rolled up. Smaller intervals provide more frequent updates but may require more computational resources.

## View products and filters

The products list shows all active products in your workspace. Archived products appear at the end of the list but are still accessible.

The list displays:
- **Product name** — Clickable to edit the product
- **Slug** — The read-only URL-friendly identifier
- **Active monitors** — Count of devices currently being monitored for this product
- **Actions** — Edit button to open the product

You can sort the product list (archived products automatically move to the end) and export all products to CSV.
