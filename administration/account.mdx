Your account settings cover your profile, password security, and personal preferences. From here you can update your identity, reset your credentials, and tailor the interface to your region and language.

## What you can do

- [**Manage your profile**](/administration/account/profile) — Edit your name, username, and view your email address.
- [**Change your password**](/administration/account/password) — Reset your password and understand the automatic logout that follows.
- [**Preferences**](/administration/account/preferences) — Set your date and time format and choose your interface language.

Your account is personal to you, but your workspace is shared. [Users](/administration/users) lets administrators manage everyone in the workspace, and the [Interface](/concepts/interface) guide helps you navigate Kapptivate's layout.
