Your account settings cover your profile, password security, and personal preferences. This page explains how to update your account details, manage your password, choose your interface language, and set date and time formats.

## Manage your profile

Your profile contains your name, username, and email address. You can edit your name and username, but your email is read-only within your profile page.

To update your profile:

1. Click your user icon at the top right of the sidebar
2. Select "My account"
3. Scroll to the Profile section
4. Edit your first name, last name, or username
5. Click "Save Changes"

Your username must be unique. The system checks availability as you type, pausing briefly before validating to avoid redundant checks.

## Change your password

You can reset your password from your account page at any time.

1. Go to My account
2. Scroll to "Account and Password"
3. Click "Reset Password"
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter your new password and confirm it
6. Click "Confirm"

After you change your password, you'll be logged out automatically in 2 seconds and returned to the login page. You can then log back in with your new password.

## Set your date and time format

Your date and time format preference applies across the entire application, helping you work with dates that match your region and preference.

1. Click your user icon at the top right
2. Select "My account"
3. Scroll to "Date & Time Format"
4. Choose your preferred date format from the dropdown (e.g., DD/MM/YYYY, MM/DD/YYYY, or YYYY-MM-DD)
5. Choose your preferred time format (e.g., 24-hour or 12-hour with AM/PM)
6. Click "Save Changes"

Your changes apply immediately to all dates and times shown in the application.

## Choose your interface language

You can set your preferred language for the interface. Changing your language instantly updates the entire application.

1. Go to My account
2. Scroll to "Language"
3. Select your preferred language from the dropdown (English or French)
4. Click "Save Changes"

The interface refreshes immediately with your selected language.
