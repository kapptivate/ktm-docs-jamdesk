Your account settings cover your identity, your team and product access, your credentials, and your personal preferences. From here you can update who you are, reset your password, and tailor the interface to your region and language.

## Open your account settings

Click your workspace name at the top of the sidebar and select **Settings**. Your personal pages sit under **My account** in the settings sidebar:

- **Profile**: your identity, teams, product access, security policy, personal access token, and password.
- **Preferences**: date and time format, and interface language.

On the Profile page, an anchor menu on the left jumps between sections: My profile, My teams, My access, Security policy, Personal access token, and Account & password.

## What you can do

- [**Manage your profile**](/administration/account/profile): edit your name and username, review your teams and product access, and copy your personal access token.
- [**Change your password**](/administration/account/password): reset your password and understand the automatic logout that follows.
- [**Preferences**](/administration/account/preferences): set your date and time format and choose your interface language.

Your account is personal to you, but your workspace is shared. [Users](/administration/users) lets administrators manage everyone in the workspace, and the [Interface](/concepts/interface) guide helps you navigate Kapptivate's layout.
