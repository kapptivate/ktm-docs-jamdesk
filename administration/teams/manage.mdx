---
title: Search and manage
description: Find teams quickly, view team details, duplicate existing teams, or delete teams you no longer need.
---

The teams list gives you a card grid with search, detailed team cards, and quick actions for duplicating or deleting teams.

## Filter and search teams

- **Search** — Enter a team name to filter (updates in real-time)
- **Card display** — Shows team name, member count, and product icons with a tooltip showing full product list

## View team details

Each team card displays:

- **Team name** — Click to edit
- **Member count** — Icon and number of members
- **Products** — Icon list showing assigned products; hover to see full names if truncated
- **Action menu** — Edit, Duplicate, Delete options

## Duplicate a team

You can clone an existing team to create a new one with the same members, products, and roles, then customize it as needed.

1. Go to Administration > Teams
2. Hover over a team card to reveal the action menu
3. Click "Duplicate"
4. The system creates a new team with a slightly modified name (e.g., "Team Copy")
5. Edit the new team to customize its name, members, or access

Duplicating is useful when you need a team with a similar structure but different scope.

## Delete a team

Deleting a team removes it permanently and cancels its product access for all members. This action cannot be undone.

1. Go to Administration > Teams
2. Hover over a team card to reveal the action menu
3. Click "Delete"
4. Confirm in the alert dialog

Members lose access to products they inherited from the deleted team. They retain access if they have direct product assignments or belong to other teams.
