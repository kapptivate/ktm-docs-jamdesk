When you create a team, you add members, assign products, and set roles that everyone in the team will have.

## Create a team

1. Go to Administration > Teams
2. Click "+ Create team"
3. In the About section, enter a team name
4. In the Members section:
   - Click "+ Add user" to open the "Add users to this team" picker
   - Search by name or email, tick the users you want (or use "Select all"), and click "Add (X)"
5. In the Access section:
   - Click "+ Add product" and pick one or more products from the modal
   - Use "Select role(s)" to choose the roles members get on those products
6. Click "Create team"

A team needs at least one product before you can create it. All members you add inherit the roles and products you assign to the team.
