You can update team details, add or remove members, or change product and role assignments at any time.

## Edit a team

1. Go to Administration > Teams
2. Click the team card to open its edit page
3. Make your changes:
   - **About**: update the team name (slug cannot be changed)
   - **Members**: add or remove team members
   - **Access**: change assigned products or roles
4. Click "Save Changes"

Changes take effect immediately. Members instantly inherit any new products or roles you assign.
