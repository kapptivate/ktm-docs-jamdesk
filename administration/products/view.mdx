The products list shows all active products in your workspace. Archived products appear at the end of the list but are still accessible.

## Products list

The list displays:
- **Product name**: the products in your workspace, with the total count in the header
- **Slug**: the read-only URL-friendly identifier
- **Active monitorings**: how many monitorings run on the product (or `-` if none)

Click a row to open the product's edit page. Archived products appear grayed at the end of the list but are still accessible. Click **Export .CSV** to download the full product list as a CSV file.
