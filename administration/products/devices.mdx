Devices are the phones, SIM cards, and agents you use for testing. Linking a device to a product makes it available for tests on that product.

## Add devices to a product

1. Open the product for editing
2. Scroll to the Allocated devices section
3. Click "Add device"
4. In the modal, switch between the SIMs, Smartphones, and Web Agents tabs and tick the devices you want (search by name or ID, filter by host, or use "Select all")
5. Click "Save"
6. Click "Save changes"

You can assign smartphones, SIM cards, and web agents to the same product.
