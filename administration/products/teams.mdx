---
title: Assign teams
description: Grant teams access to a product so all team members can work with it.
---

Team members need product access to work with a product. You can assign teams to grant all members access at once.

## Assign teams to a product

1. Open the product for editing
2. Scroll to the Teams section
3. Click "Add teams"
4. In the modal, select one or more teams
5. Click "Confirm"
6. Click "Save Changes"

Team members inherit this product access automatically.
