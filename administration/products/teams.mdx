![The Team access section of a product, listing the teams with access along with their member counts and the Add team button](/images/product-teams.webp)

Team members need product access to work with a product. You can assign teams to grant all members access at once. The Teams access table lists each assigned team with its **Members** count and an **Access** column showing how many products the team can reach; remove a team with the icon at the end of its row.

## Assign teams to a product

1. Open the product for editing
2. Scroll to the Teams access section
3. Click "+ Add team"
4. In the modal, select one or more teams
5. Confirm your selection
6. Click "Save changes"

Team members inherit this product access automatically.
