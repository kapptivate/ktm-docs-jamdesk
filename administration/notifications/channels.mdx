---
title: Configure notification channels
description: Set up email, SMS, Slack, Teams, and webhook channels for alerts.
---

Notification channels control where Kapptivate delivers alerts. Each channel can be toggled on or off independently -- enable the ones you need and fill in their required settings.

## Email

Send alerts to one or more email addresses. Select email recipients from the list of available addresses in your workspace.

## SMS

Receive alerts as text messages. Provide the phone numbers of SMS recipients. Requires a valid phone number format.

## Slack

Integrate with Slack to receive alerts in your workspace. Provide a Slack webhook URL. Kapptivate will post a message to the channel you configure in your Slack app.

## Microsoft Teams

Integrate with Microsoft Teams to receive alerts. Provide a Microsoft Teams webhook URL. Kapptivate will post a message to the Teams channel you configure.

## Webhook

Send alerts to a custom HTTP endpoint you control. Provide a webhook URL that Kapptivate will POST to when a robot becomes unavailable.

## Save and validation

Click **Save** to apply your notification settings. The save button is disabled if no changes have been made or if your configuration is invalid.

Validation requires:

- If robot availability monitoring is on, at least one notification channel must be enabled
- Email recipients must be valid email addresses
- SMS recipients must be provided (phone number format)
- Slack, Teams, and webhook URLs must be valid URLs

If validation fails, Kapptivate shows which field has the error. Fix the issue and save again.

<Info>
Unsaved changes trigger a confirmation if you navigate away from the page.
</Info>
