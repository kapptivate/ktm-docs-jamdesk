---
title: Enable robot availability monitoring
description: Track when robots go offline and configure alert detection periods.
---

Robot availability monitoring tracks when robots go offline and alerts you before downtime affects your workflows. Enable it from the **Robot Availability** toggle in your notification settings.

## Set the alert detection period

The alert detection period controls how long a robot can be offline before Kapptivate sends an alert. Choose from 5 to 30 minutes in 5-minute increments.

A shorter period catches issues faster but may trigger alerts during brief, expected downtime (such as restarts). A longer period reduces noise but delays your response to genuine outages.

## Channel requirement

When robot availability monitoring is enabled, you must configure at least one notification channel. Kapptivate will not let you save your settings without an active channel to deliver the alert.

See [notification channels](/administration/notifications/channels) to set up email, SMS, Slack, Teams, or webhook delivery.
