---
title: Metric
description: A measurement captured during a test or monitor run, such as response time or success status.
---

A **Metric** is a measurement captured when a [test](/concepts/test) or [monitor](/concepts/monitor) runs, such as a response time or a success status. Metrics turn raw runs into data you can track and chart over time, and they apply to both tests and monitors.

Some metrics are built in, for example USSD session-opening time or a mobile app's load time. You can also define [custom metrics](/tests/actions/shared/createmetric) for whatever you need to measure.

Every step produces a duration, and every step group produces one too, so timing data is available at each level of a test without extra setup.
