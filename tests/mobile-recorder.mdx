---
title: Mobile app recorder
description: Record a smartphone test by driving a real device from your browser.
---

The mobile app recorder turns a manual session on a real device into a test. You drive the smartphone from your browser through remote control, every tap and input is captured as a step, and one click generates the JavaScript test.

## Record a test

In Testing, open the **Remote control** tab and pick the smartphone to record on.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the recording">
    Click the **start a test record** icon.

    <Frame caption="The record icon in the remote control toolbar">
      ![Start a test record icon](/images/recorders/mobile-01.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the app">
    Pick the app the test will run on.

    <Frame caption="App selection">
      ![Select the app](/images/recorders/mobile-02.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Perform the steps">
    Drive the device manually. Each interaction adds a row to the recording panel.

    <Frame caption="Each interaction becomes a recorded step">
      ![Recorded steps](/images/recorders/mobile-03.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the test">
    Stop the recording (yellow icon) and click **Generate JavaScript test**. After saving, the test appears in your test list, ready to run.

    <Frame caption="Stop and generate the test">
      ![Generate JavaScript test](/images/recorders/mobile-04.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fine-tune the selectors

Each recorded step targets an element through an XPath, and the recorder picks one among several candidates. On complex apps, the automatic choice sometimes breaks on replay. Use **target mode** to choose better:

<Frame caption="The target mode icon">
  ![Start target mode](/images/recorders/mobile-05.webp)
</Frame>

Tap the element on the device screen: a popup lists every XPath that matches.

<Frame caption="The candidate XPaths for the selected element">
  ![XPath candidates](/images/recorders/mobile-06.webp)
</Frame>

Pick the shortest, clearest one, for example `//*[contains(@text,"1")]`. You can also browse the app's XML tree manually. Then open the three-dots menu next to the XPath and pick the action for the step:

<Frame caption="Choosing the action for the selected element">
  ![Action menu](/images/recorders/mobile-07.webp)
</Frame>

The most used actions:

- **Click**: tap a button or element.
- **Set value**: type text into a field.
- **Scroll until**: scroll to reach a specific element.
- **Assert text**: verify the displayed text matches what you expect.

<Note>
  The recorder generates a [smartphone script action](/tests/actions/legacy/script-actions-smartphone): JavaScript you can edit, extend with assertions, or enrich with [variables and custom metrics](/tests/actions/legacy/script-actions-variables).
</Note>
